Roasted Salmon

Description

One of the most delicious ways to prepare salmon. It gets a nice crust on the seared side and remains moist inside. On top of all that, it is beautiful to present on a plate as well.

Ingredients

  • Atlantic salmon
  • Olive oil
  • Tarragon
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Chop chives.
  2. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
  3. Rub salmon all over with 1 teaspoon oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Roast, skin side down, on a foil-lined baking sheet in upper third of oven until fish is just cooked through, about 12 minutes.
  5. Cut salmon in half crosswise, then lift flesh from skin with a metal spatula and transfer to a plate.
  6. Discard skin, then drizzle salmon with oil and sprinkle with herbs.
