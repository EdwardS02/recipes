Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • Egg white
  • Olive oil
  • Mixed vegetable
  • Egg
  • Brown rice

Steps

  1. Cook rice: combine one part rice to around 2 parts water. Bring to boil, reduce heat to low and cover, cooking until rice is tender.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the cooked rice. Scramble the egg whites and whole egg together in a bowl and add to rice. Mix well.
  3. Add your favorite mixed vegetables.
  4. Cook until heated all the way through. Optional: add a little soy sauce if it's too dry, or some chicken to make it a full meal.
